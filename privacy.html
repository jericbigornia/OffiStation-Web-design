Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how OffiStation handles and protects user information.

Note: OffiStation is an academic project developed by BS Office Administration students and does not collect, store, or share real personal data.

1. Information We Collect

OffiStation does not collect or store any sensitive personal information. Any login or account features included in the website are for demonstration only and do not store actual user data in a database.

2. Use of Information

Any information entered by users (such as names or emails in forms) is used only for interface demonstration and is not saved, processed, or shared in any way.

3. Cookies and Tracking

OffiStation does not use cookies, tracking tools, or analytics to collect user activity. All interactions on the site are handled locally within the browser for educational purposes only.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any information to third parties. Since no real data is collected, there is nothing shared outside of this academic project.

5. Data Security

Although the website simulates features like login and add-to-cart, OffiStation does not store information on any server. Therefore, no actual security risks are present, as no real data is being handled.

6. Educational Purpose Only

OffiStation is a prototype designed for learning web development concepts using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It should not be used for real shopping, user registration, or business transactions.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update or revise this Privacy Policy as we improve the project for academic purposes. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or about the OffiStation project, you may contact the project creators through the information provided on our Contact page.
