About OffiStation

OffiStation is a mini e-commerce website created to provide a convenient and organized way to browse office supplies and stationery products online.

Our Story

This project was developed by Pateros Technological College BSOA 4OL students who aim to combine their knowledge in business management with modern digital tools. Through OffiStation, we showcase our growing skills in web design and basic front-end development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with our ability to plan and organize an online store layout using Canva.

OffiStation is a school-based prototype and does not process real purchases, but it reflects our dedication to learning how online business systems work. Our goal is to continue improving the project by adding more features and exploring backend technologies in the future.
